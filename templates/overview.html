Project Overview

Addressing health challenges through cultural preservation and AI innovation.

Combating rising obesity rates in Malaysia

Problem Statement

The Need for Dietary Monitoring

Malaysia is facing a rise in diet-related health conditions, including obesity, diabetes, and heart disease. While monitoring calorie intake is crucial, manual methods like food diaries are cumbersome and prone to error.

Current solutions often lack support for traditional Malaysian Kuih. Visual similarity between kuih types and diverse textures makes recognition challenging, creating a gap in local health tools.

Impact

Project Significance

Why this project matters to society, culture, and economy.

Cultural Heritage

Repackaging traditional kuih knowledge for the younger generation to prevent culinary arts from being lost.

Public Health

Supporting KKM's obesity reduction campaigns by providing accurate, automated calorie estimations for local foods.

Education

A digital resource for culinary students and a dataset benchmark for academic research in Malaysian food recognition.

Economy

Empowering small-scale vendors in the East Coast by digitalizing and promoting their traditional products.

The Solution

AI-Powered Recognition

This project leverages Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to automate the recognition of traditional Kuih. The system extracts visual features to identify specific types with high accuracy.

  • Automated Calorie Mapping: Directly links recognized food to official KKM nutritional data.
  • Cultural Preservation: Digitizes knowledge of traditional East Coast delicacies.
